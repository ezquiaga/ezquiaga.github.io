Join us

At the GWexplorers group we always welcome expressions of interest to join our team. We strive to build a talented, dynamic and diverse team to advance in our understanding of the Universe using Gravitational Waves. The Niels Bohr Institute is at the forefront of Gravity and Astrophysics research, situated in the beatiful Copenhagen.

Below you can find a list of opportunities depending on your career stage. If you have questions do not hesitate to contact jose.ezquiaga@nbi.ku.dk.

Postdoctoral opportunities
  • Marie-Curie Fellowships
    • These are two-year postdoctoral fellowships for individuals to pursue their own research project.
  • Carlsverg Foundation
    • Reintegration Fellowships are two-year postdoctoral fellowships at Danish research institutions for postdoctoral fellows subsequent to an Internationalisation Fellowship from the Carlsberg Foundation or another research stay abroad.
